Juniper-Specific Lessons

NRE Labs is sponsored by Juniper Networks, Inc. What does this mean? In short, it means Juniper paid for the engineering time and the cloud bills to make it all possible.

However, NRE Labs is not and will not be a Juniper product pitch. Juniper Networks has plenty of other resources available for customers to explore Juniper products in detail. The workflow is king here, not a product or platform.

That said, there are some lessons which do apply mostly or only to Juniper infrastructure. In the interest of keeping everything above board, we provide disclaimers on each lesson when this is true. Even for these lessons, however, the workflow is king. You won't see technology presented for its own sake. Even for Juniper-specific lessons, the workflow will fit firmly within the overall path to Network Reliability Engineering, as one option among many.

We hope this makes it a little more clear when a lesson uses a particular workflow that can only be achieved using Juniper's automation tools. We will strive to meet two primary expectations for these lessons:

  • The workflow is king. No isolated product pitches - everything fits into a greater context of working towards NRE.
  • Whenever possible, these lessons will provide links to other lessons or resources where alternatives can be used, and provide pros/cons of each approach.
